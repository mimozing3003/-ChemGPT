// FAQ

Common questions

What exactly can ChemGPT do? add
ChemGPT can explain any chemistry concept, solve problems step-by-step, draw Lewis structures, visualize 3D molecules, predict reactions, fetch live data from PubChem, help with lab procedures, generate study notes, and act as an AI tutor — all through a single conversational interface.
Is it accurate for university-level chemistry? add
Yes. ChemGPT is trained on peer-reviewed chemistry literature and cross-references PubChem and ChEMBL databases in real time. It handles everything from high school stoichiometry to graduate-level quantum chemistry and drug discovery workflows.
Can I use it for my research paper? add
Absolutely. ChemGPT provides citations with every factual claim, integrates with PubChem and ChEMBL, and can generate synthesis protocols, property tables, and reaction mechanisms in publication-ready format.
Who built ChemGPT? add
ChemGPT was designed and built by Saumojit Roy — an engineer passionate about making chemistry education and research accessible to everyone through AI. The platform combines modern AI models with real-time chemical databases and interactive visualizations.
Is there a free tier? add
